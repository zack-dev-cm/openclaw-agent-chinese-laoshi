# Lesson 13: Large Numbers, Money, Dates, and Experience

## Lesson Summary

In this lesson, the lesson centers on Chinese large numbers pivot on 万 and 亿, Big to small order governs dates, and Sentence order stays subject, time, place, action, object and usable language includes ten thousand, one hundred thousand, one million, and one hundred million.

## Conspect Notes

- Do not let lesson 13 dissolve into abstract explanation. Its real value is practical compression: big numbers, dates, aspect, and time-sequence phrases that the learner can reuse immediately.
- Keep 万 and 亿 operational. The learner does not need every huge number in the world, but they do need the Chinese grouping logic to stop getting lost around money and dates.
- Use 了 versus 过 as a meaning contrast, not a terminology lecture. Result versus experience is enough for this stage.

## Teacher Goal

Use the lesson to automate Chinese large numbers pivot on 万 and 亿, Big to small order governs dates, and Sentence order stays subject, time, place, action, object and turn 万 (ten thousand), 十万 (one hundred thousand), and 百万 (one million) into live spoken material.

## Student Outcome

By the end of the lesson, the learner should complete the lesson mission without reading and reuse at least one current pattern in a new personal line.

- 九 / 酒: Lesson 13 explicitly uses nine versus alcohol as a tone-meaning contrast. Keep the segment pair together in practice.
- Do not let lesson 13 dissolve into abstract explanation. Its real value is practical compression: big numbers, dates, aspect, and time-sequence phrases that the learner can reuse immediately.
