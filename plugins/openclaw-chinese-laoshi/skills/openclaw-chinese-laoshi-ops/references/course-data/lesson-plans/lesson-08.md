# Lesson 08: Meeting People, Age, and Quantity Questions

## Lesson Summary

In this lesson, the lesson centers on 认识 versus 知道, Age uses 岁, not 年, and Quantity question with 多少 and usable language includes glad; happy, to know, in the sense of having information, to know someone; to meet; to become acquainted, and glad to meet you.

## Conspect Notes

- Keep lesson 08 social first. 高兴认识你 and the two 'know' verbs immediately make the learner sound more natural in conversation.
- Use age and quantity together in one block. 岁, 多少, and 多大 are easier to retain when contrasted directly.
- Treat result-complement pairs as upgrades of familiar verbs: 看 becomes 看到, 听 becomes 听到, 找 becomes 找到.

## Teacher Goal

Use the lesson to automate 认识 versus 知道, Age uses 岁, not 年, and Quantity question with 多少 and turn 高兴 (glad; happy), 知道 (to know, in the sense of having information), and 认识 (to know someone; to meet; to become acquainted) into live spoken material.

## Student Outcome

By the end of the lesson, the learner should complete the lesson mission without reading and reuse at least one current pattern in a new personal line.

## Teacher Question Ladder

- 你认识他吗？ — ni renshi ta ma? — Do you know him? Are you acquainted with him?
- 你多大？ — ni duo da? — How old are you? Literally: how big are you?
- 你几岁？ — ni ji sui? — How old are you? for a child
- 你什么时候睡觉？ — ni shenme shihou shuijiao? — When do you go to sleep?
- 你今天晚上做什么？ — ni jintian wanshang zuo shenme? — What will you do this evening?

## Correction Priorities

- 高兴认识你: Treat 高兴认识你 as a smooth social chunk. If you pause after each syllable, it stops sounding like a greeting.
- 多少 / 多大: Keep 多少 and 多大 distinct: both start with 多, but the second syllable changes the meaning completely.
- Keep lesson 08 social first. 高兴认识你 and the two 'know' verbs immediately make the learner sound more natural in conversation.
- Use age and quantity together in one block. 岁, 多少, and 多大 are easier to retain when contrasted directly.
- If the learner breaks the frame, shrink back to one stable sentence and rebuild one variable at a time.
