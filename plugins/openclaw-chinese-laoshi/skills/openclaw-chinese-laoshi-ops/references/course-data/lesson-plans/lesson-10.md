# Lesson 10: Clothing, Colors, and Shopping Classifiers

## Lesson Summary

In this lesson, the lesson centers on Clothing often takes specific classifiers, not just 个, Long items use 条, and Color attaches to nouns through 色的 or direct modifier position and usable language includes clothes; clothing, things; items; stuff, to shop; to buy things, and to wear; to put on.

## Conspect Notes

- Do not let lesson 10 turn into a word list. Keep classifiers and clothing nouns bound together as one operational system.
- Use 件, 双, and 条 as the memorable skeleton of the lesson. That gives the learner a practical reason to care about measure words.
- Anchor the color material in real objects: shirt, clothes, and car. This is what turns lesson 09 color words into useful speech.

## Teacher Goal

Use the lesson to automate Clothing often takes specific classifiers, not just 个, Long items use 条, and Color attaches to nouns through 色的 or direct modifier position and turn 衣服 (clothes; clothing), 东西 (things; items; stuff), and 买东西 (to shop; to buy things) into live spoken material.

## Student Outcome

By the end of the lesson, the learner should complete the lesson mission without reading and reuse at least one current pattern in a new personal line.

## Correction Priorities

- 件 / 双 / 条: Keep the clothing classifiers distinct. If you blur them together, the clothing block collapses into noise.
- 穿: 穿 should stay short and stable in clothing sentences. It is one of the central verbs of the lesson.
- Do not let lesson 10 turn into a word list. Keep classifiers and clothing nouns bound together as one operational system.
- Use 件, 双, and 条 as the memorable skeleton of the lesson. That gives the learner a practical reason to care about measure words.
- If the learner breaks the frame, shrink back to one stable sentence and rebuild one variable at a time.
