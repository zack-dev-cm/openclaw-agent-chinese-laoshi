# Lesson 14: Weather, Temperature, Travel, and Geography

## Lesson Summary

In this lesson, the lesson centers on Weather statements anchor on 今天 and 天气, Weather events behave like verb phrases, and Temperature combines 气温 with 零上 or 零下 and 度 and usable language includes weather, today, cold, and hot.

## Conspect Notes

- Keep lesson 14 practical. The point is not to list climate nouns in isolation but to let the learner ask, answer, and survive weather talk during travel.
- Temperature is the lesson's second backbone. Drill 零上, 零下, and 度 together until the learner can produce minus and plus temperatures without translation lag.
- Do not split the route block away from the weather block. Travel plus weather is the exact real-world use case the teacher is aiming at here.

## Teacher Goal

Use the lesson to automate Weather statements anchor on 今天 and 天气, Weather events behave like verb phrases, and Temperature combines 气温 with 零上 or 零下 and 度 and turn 天气 (weather), 今天 (today), and 冷 (cold) into live spoken material.

## Student Outcome

By the end of the lesson, the learner should complete the lesson mission without reading and reuse at least one current pattern in a new personal line.

## Teacher Question Ladder

- 今天刮风吗？ — jintian gua feng ma? — Is it windy today?
- 你去过中国吗？ — ni qu guo Zhongguo ma? — Have you ever been to China?
- 你想去哪个国家？ — ni xiang qu nage guojia? — Which country do you want to go to?
- 你要去什么地方？ — ni yao qu shenme difang? — What place are you going to go to?
- 你最喜欢哪个城市？ — ni zui xihuan nage chengshi? — Which city do you like most?

## Correction Priorities

- 天气: Keep 天气 compact as one weather chunk rather than pausing after 天.
- 刮风: The weather verb needs to stay rhythmic and compact; do not separate 刮 from 风 into two unrelated beats.
- Keep lesson 14 practical. The point is not to list climate nouns in isolation but to let the learner ask, answer, and survive weather talk during travel.
- Temperature is the lesson's second backbone. Drill 零上, 零下, and 度 together until the learner can produce minus and plus temperatures without translation lag.
- If the learner breaks the frame, shrink back to one stable sentence and rebuild one variable at a time.
