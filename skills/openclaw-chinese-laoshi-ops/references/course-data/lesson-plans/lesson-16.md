# Lesson 16: Final Biographies, Questions, and Closing Conversation

## Lesson Summary

In this lesson, the lesson centers on Final biography mode chains simple clauses, Question exchange uses 有问题吗 and live follow-ups, and 为什么 ... 因为 ... remains the compact reasoning pair and usable language includes hello everyone, you all look beautiful today, question; problem, and do you have any questions?.

## Conspect Notes

- Do not teach lesson 16 as one more topic lesson. It is the course wrap-up where the learner is supposed to speak as a person, not just fill grammar slots.
- The safest way to use this lesson is biography plus follow-up questions: name, city, family, travel, likes, work, and one toast or farewell line.
- Keep why-because, favorite color, and music preference short. They are there to keep a conversation alive, not to turn into abstract explanation blocks.

## Teacher Goal

## Student Outcome

## Correction Priorities

- 你们好 / 你们今天都很美: Keep 你们 as one fast greeting chunk and do not flatten 都很美 into word-by-word stress.
- 为什么 / 因为: These two short reasoning words need to be heard and answered as a pair without hesitation.
- Do not teach lesson 16 as one more topic lesson. It is the course wrap-up where the learner is supposed to speak as a person, not just fill grammar slots.
- The safest way to use this lesson is biography plus follow-up questions: name, city, family, travel, likes, work, and one toast or farewell line.
- If the learner breaks the frame, shrink back to one stable sentence and rebuild one variable at a time.
