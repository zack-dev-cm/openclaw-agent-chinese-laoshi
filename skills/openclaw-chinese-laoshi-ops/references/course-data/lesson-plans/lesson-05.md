# Lesson 05: Larger Numbers, Direction, and Going Somewhere

## Lesson Summary

In this lesson, the lesson centers on Hundreds and thousands use 百 and 千, 零 fills missing place values, and Directional verbs 去 and 来 and usable language includes excellent; extremely good, hundred, zero, and thousand.

## Conspect Notes

- Do not let lesson 05 become only a number chant. Tie the hundred and thousand patterns immediately to phone numbers, dates, or place counts.
- The movement block is the real hinge of the lesson: contrast 去, 来, 回, 回来, and 回家 until the learner can feel the direction without translation.
- Use 喜欢 plus cities or countries for natural conversational output instead of abstract geography quizzes.

## Teacher Goal

Use the lesson to automate Hundreds and thousands use 百 and 千, 零 fills missing place values, and Directional verbs 去 and 来 and turn 非常好 (excellent; extremely good), 百 (hundred), and 零 (zero) into live spoken material.

## Student Outcome

By the end of the lesson, the learner should complete the lesson mission without reading and reuse at least one current pattern in a new personal line.

## Petrov Stage

## Teacher Question Ladder

- 你们好吗？你们好吗？今天好吗？ — ni men hao ma? ni men hao ma? jintian hao ma? — How are you all? Are you all well? Is today going well?
- 这是什么？ — zhe shi shenme? — What is this?
- 这是谁？ — zhe shi shei? — Who is this?
- 你叫什么名字？ — ni jiao shenme mingzi? — What is your name?
- 你去过哪个国家？ — ni qu guo na ge guojia? — Which country have you been to?

## Correction Priorities

- 千 vs 钱: The lesson pairs 千 and 钱 to show that the syllable stays similar while the tone changes the meaning completely.
- 喜欢: Keep the second syllable of 喜欢 lighter and less stressed; Russian speakers often over-hit the end of the word.
- Do not let lesson 05 become only a number chant. Tie the hundred and thousand patterns immediately to phone numbers, dates, or place counts.
- The movement block is the real hinge of the lesson: contrast 去, 来, 回, 回来, and 回家 until the learner can feel the direction without translation.
- If the learner breaks the frame, shrink back to one stable sentence and rebuild one variable at a time.
