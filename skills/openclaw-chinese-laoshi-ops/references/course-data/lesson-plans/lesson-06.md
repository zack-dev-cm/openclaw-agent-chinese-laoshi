## Conspect Notes

- Keep lesson 06 practical by returning to already-known verbs and only then layering in new time words and social formulas.
- Use the contrast between 不 and 没 as the main grammar loop; it is the most useful new distinction in this lesson.
- Do not teach 吃饭, 喝茶, 睡觉, and 做饭 as isolated verbs. Keep them as whole action chunks.

Use the lesson to automate Time roots become parts of day with 上, 知道 and 吗 build a practical check-in question, and 会说 plus a language and turn 当然 (of course), 早 (early), and 晚 (late) into live spoken material.

## Teacher Question Ladder

- 你知道吗？ — ni zhidao ma? — Do you know?
- 你会说俄语吗？ — ni hui shuo Eyu ma? — Do you speak Russian?
- 你会说什么语言？ — ni hui shuo shenme yuyan? — What language can you speak?
- 你昨天晚上做什么？ — ni zuotian wanshang zuo shenme? — What did you do yesterday evening?
- 你今天晚上做什么？ — ni jintian wanshang zuo shenme? — What are you doing this evening?

## Correction Priorities

- 当然: Keep 当然 as a smooth two-syllable chunk; Russian speakers often over-separate the second syllable.
- 早上 / 晚上: Practice 早上 and 晚上 as time-of-day bundles rather than stressing each character like a list.
- Keep lesson 06 practical by returning to already-known verbs and only then layering in new time words and social formulas.
- Use the contrast between 不 and 没 as the main grammar loop; it is the most useful new distinction in this lesson.
- If the learner breaks the frame, shrink back to one stable sentence and rebuild one variable at a time.
